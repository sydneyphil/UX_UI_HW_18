My Skills

User Research

I have a passion for deeply understanding users needs, empathizing with their frustrations to help guide me towards a helpful solution. To do so, I conducted user interviews creating open ended questions and surveys to gather valuable insights.

Information Architecture

Mapping out the blueprint of a user-interface is one of my favorite stages of design. After collecting data from iterviews and defining the user, constructing the skeleton of an app or website brings the design vision to life.

Wireframing

In this stage of design, I utilized tools such as Figma to create wireframes and interactive prototypes ranging from low fidelity to high fidelity for iOS, Android, and web.
